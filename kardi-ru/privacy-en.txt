Consent is given for the processing of the following personal data of the User:

During depersonalization, the following data is removed: last name, first name, patronymic, country, city, and minor random changes may be introduced into the date of birth. Depersonalized data may be used for scientific medical research and publication in specialized medical sources.

The User’s personal data is not transferred to third parties, except for persons who process personal data on behalf of and under the instruction of the Operator, as well as cases established by law.
